<section class="interlude-dialogue" aria-label="A short dialogue between a wizard and an apprentice">
  <p><strong>Apprentice:</strong> Numbers are obedient.</p>
  <p><strong>Wizard:</strong> Until you name them.</p>
  <p><strong>Apprentice:</strong> Names make trouble?</p>
  <p><strong>Wizard:</strong> Names invite meaning.</p>
  <p><strong>Apprentice:</strong> Is meaning bad?</p>
  <p><strong>Wizard:</strong> Only when it escapes.</p>
