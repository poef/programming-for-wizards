# Acknowledgements

## A note on AI

<!-- paragraph-id: p-18-i-used-ai-extensively-while-writing-this-book -->
I used AI extensively while writing this book: as a sounding board, critic, editor and, occasionally, a source of first drafts. It helped me test arguments, find gaps and try different ways of saying things.

<!-- paragraph-id: p-18-i-did-not-publish-its-answers-as-they -->
I did not publish its answers as they arrived. I chose the ideas, rewrote the text and made the final decisions. Whatever is wrong with this book is therefore still my fault.
